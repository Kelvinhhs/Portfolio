About Me


Studying Media Arts at UCSD allowed me to pursue my design career. By completing many photography, film production, and color theory related courses. I was able to look at graphic design from other perspectives and discover different ideas for artistic creation. I was also exposed to a variety of people with different cultures, designs and aesthetics. I spent 3 years doing internship in different advertising companies in various countries. It gave me a deep understanding of what a graphic designer does in practice and gave me a lot of practical experience. Just as graphic designers communicate with their clients, they also need to discuss how to promote and design their project with their teammates. I have learned that while individual hard work is important, understanding how to cooperate with others in perfect harmony is the key to success.
